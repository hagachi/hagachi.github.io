Short talk at Public Online Symposium of Ecological Society of Japan "Mainstreaming renewable energy in decade of action requires ecologists"

Project Slides
Image credit: Chihiro Haga

Abstract

In the decade of action, we need to achieve both decarbonization & biodiversity conservation at the same time. We developed the spatially explicit renewable energy potential map in Japan. We showed some applications of this map for designing sustainable renewable energy installation. All scripts can be downloaded from our GitHub repository. https://github.com/Green-Engineers-Lab/REROUTES

Specially Appointed Researcher

Haga, Chihiro is an ecological engineer conducting a scenario analysis of nexuses between biodiversity and ecosystem services at a local scale. His research experiences include the application of the process-based forest landscape simulator for social-ecological systems modeling.
