Haga, Chihiro

Specially Appointed Researcher

GE lab, Osaka University

Ecological Engineering for Sustainability

My mission is to support a local SDGs’ achievement by applying forest & landscape ecology and data science as an engineer. Essential tools are 1) the process-based forest landscape simulation and 2) data science techs for monitoring and modeling the nexus within Social-Ecological Systems. Cooperation with researchers and stakeholders is a key for achieving the goals. A new collaboration is always welcome.
